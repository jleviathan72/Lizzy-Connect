ARTICLE 1: RECITALS
1.1 Background
WHEREAS, Leviathan is developing Lizzy Connect, an SMS-based customer workflow system for equipment dealers using Lizzy ERP, targeting the powersports, outdoor power equipment (OPE), and marine dealer industries;
WHEREAS, Carl's is an established OPE dealer with extensive experience in service operations, customer engagement, and commercial landscaping equipment sales;
WHEREAS, the parties desire to collaborate on the development, validation, and commercialization of Lizzy Connect, with Carl's serving as a co-development partner, validation site, and reference customer;
WHEREAS, Leviathan desires to engage Carl's as an active partner to provide functional use cases, operational feedback, real-world testing environment, and go-to-market support;
WHEREAS, Carl's desires to receive revenue participation, free lifetime usage of Lizzy Connect, and fractional CTO services from Leviathan in exchange for its contributions;
WHEREAS, Leviathan will provide comprehensive fractional Chief Technology Officer (CTO) services to Carl's, including both general technology leadership for Carl's business operations AND dedicated project, product, and development management for Lizzy Connect through all development phases;
WHEREAS, Carl's operates a YouTube channel featuring equipment troubleshooting and maintenance videos, and the parties anticipate that Lizzy Connect will drive increased traffic to this content, resulting in additional advertising revenue and business value for Carl's;
NOW THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements herein, the parties agree as follows:
